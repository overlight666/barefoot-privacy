# Privacy Policy for Faith Daily

**Last Updated: February 1, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Faith Daily ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Registration**: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture, church affiliation
- **Communication**: Messages, prayers, prayer requests, shares, and other content you post
- **Profile Information**: Bio, preferences, spiritual interests, and group memberships
- **Payment Information**: For premium subscriptions (processed through secure third-party payment processors)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Data**: Pages accessed, features used, time spent, search queries, interaction patterns
- **Location Data**: General location based on IP address (not GPS tracking)
- **Crash Logs**: Error reports and diagnostic information to improve the Service

### 2.3 Camera Permission
We request camera access only for:
- **Profile Picture Upload**: Allowing you to take a new photo for your church profile or personal profile
- **Logo Upload**: Enabling church administrators to capture or upload church logos

Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to use the camera feature. We do not access your camera without your permission.

### 2.4 Storage Permission
We request storage access for:
- Selecting and uploading images from your device
- Saving downloaded content locally

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery**: Creating and managing your account, delivering the Service features
- **Communication**: Sending service updates, support responses, and notifications
- **Community Features**: Enabling prayer sharing, group discussions, and church communications
- **Personalization**: Customizing your experience based on preferences and activities
- **Analytics**: Understanding usage patterns to improve our Service
- **Security**: Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, and protecting user safety
- **Legal Compliance**: Meeting legal obligations and enforcing our terms

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 Within Your Church Community
- Your church profile information, prayers, and posts are visible to other church members
- Church administrators can see member information for management purposes
- Prayer requests and shares are visible according to your privacy settings

### 4.2 With Service Providers
We share information with third-party service providers who assist us:
- **Firebase** (Google): Backend services, authentication, notifications, analytics
- **Payment Processors**: For processing subscriptions and purchases
- **Cloud Storage Providers**: For secure data storage

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our legal rights
- Prevent fraud or security issues
- Protect user safety

### 4.4 Business Transfers
If Faith Daily is involved in a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

- Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits
- Limited access to personal information

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your account at any time, and we will remove your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where required by law to maintain records.

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 7.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to access your personal information and receive a copy in a portable format.

### 7.2 Correction
You can correct or update your personal information through your account settings.

### 7.3 Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and associated personal information.

### 7.4 Opt-Out
You can opt out of non-essential communications and analytics tracking through your settings.

### 7.5 California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of personal information.

### 7.6 EU Residents (GDPR)
If you are located in the EU, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to access, correction, deletion, and data portability.

## 8. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with third-party services:

### 8.1 Google Firebase
- Authentication, database, storage, analytics, notifications
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 8.2 Payment Processors
- Secure processing of subscription and purchase payments
- Subject to their respective privacy policies

### 8.3 Analytics
- We use analytics to understand how users interact with our Service
- Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Requesting your consent if required by law

Your continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.
