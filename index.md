# Privacy Policy for Barefoot

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Barefoot ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains our online information practices and the choices you can make about how your information is used.

## Information We Collect

### 1. **Location Information**
- **What we collect:** GPS coordinates when you use location-based features to discover nearby stores and events.
- **How we use it:** To show you relevant local information and improve our service.
- **Your control:** You can enable or disable location permissions in your device settings at any time.

### 2. **Camera Information**
- **What we collect:** Camera access is only enabled when you choose to take photos or scan features.
- **How we use it:** To allow you to capture and share content within the app.
- **Your control:** You can deny camera permissions in your device settings.

### 3. **Account Information**
- **What we collect:** Email address or phone number for authentication and account creation.
- **How we use it:** To verify your identity and send you authentication codes.

### 4. **Communication Data**
- **What we collect:** Messages, preferences, and interaction data with other users.
- **How we use it:** To deliver the app's core features and improve user experience.

## How We Protect Your Information

- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.
- Authentication codes are time-limited and automatically expire after 10 minutes.
- Your account data is encrypted during transmission.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Supabase:** For database and authentication services ([Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Gmail:** For email delivery of authentication codes

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request access to your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Modify your privacy preferences in app settings
- Disable location or camera permissions at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the app.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

**Email:** solutionsbarefoot@gmail.com

## Data Retention

- We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services.
- Upon account deletion, your data is removed from our systems within 30 days.
- Authentication codes are automatically deleted after expiration.
